About

Who am I?

I am a freshly minted web developer, currently looking to work in a front-end development position. I have a background in e-commerce, consisting of building CMS components, designing and developing corporate webpages, and maintaining various internal tools. I am especially passionate about UX and UI design.

How I got Here

Prior to completing Seneca College’s Computer Programming diploma, I completed a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology at the University of Guelph. During my Bachelors, I developed a particular interest in how people interact with technology and took courses on computer systems and user interface design. Entering a new academic field with the goal of becoming a web developer, I had the opportunity to intern as a full-stack developer for Softchoice. During my co-op, I built product pages for multi-million dollar clients, redesigned internal tools and sections of the Softchoice website, and built reuseable components for a new headless CMS.
